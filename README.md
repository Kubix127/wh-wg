# Multiplayer political management strategy web game made with React, Node.js and SQL

## Concept
A turn based game for approx. 20 players with the possibility of raising that number as the game world expands. Players would take a lead of a country in a fantasy setting focusing on political play, economy management and war strategy. In addition to players competing for power, the presence of the large outside threat would force alliances and cooperation. The Game Master would oversight the game and players' decisions, while deciding how they would impact their situation (simmilar to Dungeons&Dragons). Presence of the Game Master gives to the players ability implement strategies and decisions that go beyond game mechanics.

## Features
- 2 species (Humans and Vampires) with multiple cultures to choose from
- Political map where action would take place
- City management - Tiers, buildings, decrees
- Army and units management - Units with specialisations, counters and abilities
- Realationship panel with treaties, trade and relation meter between populations of factions
- Political forum where discussions would take place - Direct and global message system between players (distance between players impacts the delay), Game Master's event handling
- Agents for each faction with the ability of political play, espionage or assisting armies
- Game mechanics - Control over region (low control would cause riots), corruption (city and army debuffs spredable by certain factions), 
- Unique interactive tech tree for each species impacting every aspect of the game
- Unique Lore units for factions within species
- Unique Lore buildings in certain locations

## Planned
